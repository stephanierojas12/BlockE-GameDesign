This is a two player game! Grab a friend and 
play a round or two of this innovative game.

The objective of the game is to try and
escape the circle while the circle tries to eat the square.

Use these keys for the circle: 
left arrow to move it left, right arrow to move it right
up arrow to move it up, and down arrow to move it down

Use these keys for the square:
A key to move left,S key to move down, D to go left, W to go up
